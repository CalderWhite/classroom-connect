    • What is Classroom Connect?

    Classroom Connect is a safe, and secure platform for connecting students around Ontario. All you need to do is choose a subject you want a study partner for, and our program will search for other students with the same classes and match you up with them. Using our built-in chat application, students will be able to discuss classwork with other students. This safe and easy to use platform will provide a place for studying, learning, and collaboration.

    About

    IN THE SHADOWS. One strength of Classroom Connect is that it is multi-platformed. Since it is web-based it can be anything that has access to the internet. This makes it accessible to almost anyone, at almost anytime! Classroom Connect is a great way to be productive even when not in the classroom, and we are planning to make OS specific software for even more widespread usage.

    We focus on simplicity, and ease of use. When signing up and logging in, all students need to do is use their pre-made Google Apps accounts. After clicking allow access, the student's account is set for using our service. After being signed up, all of the student's class information is layed out in front of them; no hassle at all. Then, with a few simple clicks they can begin to search for their perfect study partners. This makes Classroom connect accessible to all people of all ages.

    Lastly, we assure only the greatest Safety and Security for our users. Most websites allow you to make an account and log-in with only an (sometimes even fake) email address. However, we require a Google Apps for Education account in order to sign in, preventing unauthorized and potentially dangerous people from trying to communicate with our users. This will reduce the amount of harassments and spam.
